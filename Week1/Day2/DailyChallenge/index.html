THOR ODINSON

A hero's welcome to Asgard, Thor was forced to return to Earth to retrieve Loki after he survived to fall into a black hole and began his bid for world domination, having taken possession of the Tesseract. He joined the Avengers under S.H.I.E.L.D. and stood with them to stop his own adopted brother's plans, eventually following the newly formed team in their fight against the Chitauri army during the Battle of New York until his brother was finally defeated. by the Avengers, captured and returned to Asgard to seek justice for his crimes against Earth.
